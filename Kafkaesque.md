# Kafkaesque

>[!Definition]
>**Adjective**: Extremely nightmarish, dystopian, and unpleasant; reminiscent of Franz Kafka's literature

**Example**
- When I'm feeling anxious, I mentally picture the Kafkaesque work and life culture in my own country
